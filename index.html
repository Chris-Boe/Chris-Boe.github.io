    Piano Lessons

    Teaching beginner piano lessons to kids and avid learners in the Pacific Northwest!

    Curriculum:

    I have played piano for 15 years now and have loved (nearly) every minute of it! Performing and accompanying in recitals, competitions, and shows have kept me busy and dedicated. Starting at a young age is the best time to begin fostering a fun, social, and life-long hobby or more! Taking music lessons at some point in life is highly recommended and I couldn't agree more. Since high-school, I have enjoyed sharing my knowledge of music and piano with friends and students in my home community. Moving on from high-school, I took on new students in Tacoma, Washington. With students ranging from age 6-20, it's a learning experience for all!

    • For lessons, I use a variety of books from the Alfred series encompassing both music theory and piano technique.
    • I am located in the Tacoma, Washington area and teach those in the local.
    • In most cases I use the music rooms at Pacific Lutheran University to teach (the pianos are superb!), however house calls can be arranged.
    • You can find my email in the contact me section on this site for lesson and pay rate queries!
